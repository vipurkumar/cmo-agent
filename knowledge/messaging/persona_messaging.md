# Persona-Specific Messaging Guide

Tailored messaging for each buying role in the OmniGTM sales process. Each persona entry includes a pain hook, value statement, proof point, and email opener. Adapt tone and specifics to the prospect's company and context.

## VP Revenue Operations

**Pain Hook**: "Your team is spending more time reconciling billing data and fixing pricing errors than optimizing the revenue engine."

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM eliminates the manual billing operations work — usage reconciliation, invoice generation, entitlement tracking, pricing error cleanup — so your RevOps team can focus on what actually moves revenue: pipeline optimization, forecasting accuracy, and GTM alignment. One unified pricing engine replaces the spreadsheet-and-prayer approach to multi-product billing.

**Proof Point**: DataFlow Analytics reduced billing operations from 2 FTEs to 0.4 FTE (80% reduction), eliminated $340K in annual revenue leakage, and cut quote-to-cash cycle from 22 days to 7 days — freeing the RevOps team to focus on growth initiatives.

**Email Opener**: "I was looking at how {{company}} structures pricing across your product lines, and I'm curious — how much of your RevOps team's time goes into billing reconciliation and fixing pricing errors? We helped DataFlow Analytics take that from 70+ hours a week to 14, while recovering $340K in revenue they didn't know they were leaking. Worth a 15-minute conversation?"
