# Approved Value Propositions

Organized by pain type. Use these in outbound sequences, landing pages, and sales collateral. Each entry includes the pain type, headline, value statement, and proof point.

---

## Pain Type: Manual GTM Execution

**Headline**: Stop building outbound by hand. Let AI agents do the research, writing, and sending.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM replaces 14+ hours per week of manual list building, account research, and email personalization with AI agents that run your entire outbound workflow — from ICP scoring to personalized send — while your team focuses on closing.

**Proof Point**: Acme SaaS reduced deal desk cycle time by 52% and increased personalized outbound volume 3.4x within 90 days of deployment.

---

## Pain Type: Low Reply Rates

**Headline**: Generic outbound is dead. Personalization at scale is not.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM researches every account — news, job postings, tech stack, pricing model — and generates emails that reference specific, timely pain points. No more "Hi {first_name}, I noticed your company..." templates.

**Proof Point**: FinTech Co increased reply rates from 1.8% to 6.7% (3.7x) by switching from template-based to AI-personalized outbound sequences.

---

## Pain Type: Inconsistent Messaging

**Headline**: One source of truth for every email your team sends.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM's knowledge base ensures every AI-generated email uses approved value propositions, competitive positioning, and objection responses. No more rogue messaging from new BDRs or outdated battlecards.

**Proof Point**: FinTech Co achieved 89% knowledge base reference rate across all generated emails, ensuring consistent messaging across 14 BDRs.

---

## Pain Type: CRM Data Decay

**Headline**: Your CRM is only as good as last quarter. OmniGTM keeps it current.

**Value Statement**: Continuous enrichment from Apollo, Clay, LinkedIn, and web scraping keeps contact records, job titles, and company data fresh without manual effort. No more bounced emails to people who left 6 months ago.

**Proof Point**: Acme SaaS reduced stale CRM records from 38% to 11% with automated continuous enrichment.

---

## Pain Type: Scaling Outbound Without Scaling Headcount

**Headline**: 10x your outbound without 10x your team.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM runs 10,000+ concurrent sessions at under $0.08 LLM cost per session. Each AI agent does the work of a full-time BDR — research, personalization, sequencing, and follow-up — so you can scale pipeline without scaling payroll.

**Proof Point**: Acme SaaS generated $1.2M in influenced pipeline in 90 days with zero additional headcount.

---

## Pain Type: Multi-Persona Selling

**Headline**: Reach every buyer in the committee, with the message they care about.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM automatically detects buyer personas (Engineering, Finance, Product, RevOps) and routes each contact into persona-specific sequences with tailored messaging. One campaign, multiple buying committee members, all personalized.

**Proof Point**: FinTech Co increased multi-persona coverage from 28% to 73% of target accounts within the first sequence cycle.

---

## Pain Type: Slow Sales Cycles

**Headline**: Compress your sales cycle by educating prospects before the first call.

**Value Statement**: OmniGTM's AI agents deliver relevant case studies, technical content, and competitive comparisons in the outbound sequence itself — so prospects arrive at the first call informed and ready to evaluate, not just curious.

**Proof Point**: FinTech Co reduced average sales cycle from 67 days to 46 days (31% reduction) with AI-sequenced buyer education.
